## Row

## Row (default)

Rows have a defined bottom margin

    @example
    <div class="uicc-row">
        Row 1
    </div>
    <div class="uicc-row">
        Row 2
    </div>
    <div class="uicc-row">
        Row 3
    </div>

## Row (inverse)

Inverse rows have a top margin instead of a bottom margin

    @example
    <div class="uicc-row uicc-row--inverse">
        Row 1
    </div>
    <div class="uicc-row uicc-row--inverse">
        Row 2
    </div>
    <div class="uicc-row uicc-row--inverse">
        Row 3
    </div>
