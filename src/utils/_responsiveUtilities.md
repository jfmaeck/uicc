### Scss Media Query Breakpoint variables

Commonly used media query conditions exposed as scss variables:
`$uicc-tiny-only`
`$uicc-tiny-up`
`$uicc-small-only`
`$uicc-small-up`
`$uicc-medium-only
`$uicc-medium-up`
`$uicc-large-only`
`$uicc-large-up`
